# 💳 Credit Card Installment Strategy Analysis using the Olist E-Commerce Dataset

## 📌 Project Objective

This project analyzes customer credit card installment behavior using the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to understand how financing duration influences revenue across different product categories.

Using SQL for data preparation and Python for visualization, the project explores customer payment behavior, installment usage, and category-level revenue to identify financing strategies that maximize revenue while maintaining efficient installment policies.

The analysis demonstrates that longer installment periods do not necessarily generate higher revenue and suggests that installment strategies should be customized according to product categories rather than applied uniformly across the platform.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Installment payments increase customers' purchasing power but delay business cash flow. The objective of this project is to identify which product categories justify extended financing and which categories unnecessarily lock business capital.

This project answers the following business questions:

- Which payment methods generate the highest revenue?
- How do customers use installment plans?
- Do higher-value purchases require longer financing?
- Which product categories generate the highest credit card revenue?
- Which categories rely most on installment financing?
- How can financing strategies be optimized at the product-category level?

# ⚙️ Data Engineering Highlights

Before performing business analysis, the dataset was carefully validated to ensure reliable insights.

Key validation steps included:

- Missing value analysis
- Duplicate record validation
- Installment data verification
- Product category translation validation
- Prevention of revenue inflation caused by one-to-many joins using SQL pre-aggregation

These steps ensured that all revenue calculations and business insights were based on accurate and consistent data.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- **Health Beauty** generates high credit card revenue while maintaining below-median installment duration, indicating efficient financing performance.

- **Watches Gifts** and **Bed, Bath & Table** achieve high revenue despite above-median installment durations, suggesting that longer financing can support selected high-performing categories.

- Lower-revenue categories with longer installment durations contribute comparatively less revenue while extending financing periods, making them suitable candidates for reviewing financing or promotional strategies.

- Financing duration alone does not determine business performance. Product category demand plays a much stronger role in driving revenue.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize marketing and inventory investment for consistently high-performing categories such as **Health Beauty** and **Watches Gifts**.

- Replace uniform installment policies with **category-specific financing strategies** based on customer purchasing behavior.

- Review lower-performing categories requiring longer installment periods to determine whether pricing, financing terms, or promotional strategies should be adjusted.

- Future analysis should incorporate repayment behavior, profitability, and customer retention data to evaluate the long-term effectiveness of installment financing.
